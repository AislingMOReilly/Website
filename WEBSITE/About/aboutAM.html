About Me


Qualifications


I qualified as a Career Coach through the Dublin Institute of Technology. I hold a BSc(Hons) in Psychology and am a member of the British Psychological Society. I am also a qualified Psychometrics Tester in a wide range of Psychometric Assessments. I have 20 years’ experience coaching and developing people in multiple management roles. I have career coached clients from diverse backgrounds and occupations as well as clients about to retire helping them successfully transition to the next phase of their lives.




Experience


In addition to Career Coaching and people development experience, I can provide the benefits of real world experience from my former roles including Operations and Change Management, Print and Supply Chain, Process Design and Improvement, HR, Banking, IT and Training and Communications. Such a range and depth of experience gives me unique insight into what employers are looking for across a wide range of roles. As a result, I can help clients to identify and match what in their existing experience makes them uniquely qualified for the role they are now considering or would like to consider, and how to use and express that knowledge confidently and effectively.



My coaching style is open, friendly, supportive and empathic. You will be ‘wowed’ by your new CV; you will be able to face all types of interviews feeling prepared and confident; you will impress widely with your updated social media/LinkedIn profile. I am passionate about helping clients increase in confidence, helping them in learning to identify, value and appreciate the skills, talents and experience they have and helping them express this effectively and professionally. This leads clients to focused and practical solutions.
