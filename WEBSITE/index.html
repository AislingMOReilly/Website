What is Career Coaching?



Career coaching is a process that empowers clients to discover the careers they will find most satisfying. If you are happy in your work, it positively impacts all areas of your life. Career Coaching will help you make better choices that will be more personally rewarding. You gain new insight and appreciation of what you offer potential employers. Career coaching provides the skills needed at this crucial time and an ability to express your achievements, experience and aspirations effectively.



The relationship between Coach and Client is collaborative, bringing about self-awareness, understanding and appreciation of the clients’ individual talents and skills. Coaching addresses all clients’ needs at a time when they are considering a change or are unsure about which direction they should take next e.g. a world class CV, expert Interview Skills, Job Search Strategies, Networking Know-how, and more.
